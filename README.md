# 🏕️ Donegal + Sligo Camping Trip

**Mon 6 July → Sat 11 July 2026** · 5 nights, 2 of us, 1 car

---

## The plan in a sentence

Newbridge → Donegal Town (2 nights) → Slieve League cliffs (1 night) → Sligo (2 nights) → home. Days outdoors hiking, swimming and beach-hopping. Evenings in lively pub towns for trad music and pints.

**Daily rhythm:** Breakfast and slow start, on the road ~10-11am. One early start (5am) for Slieve League sunrise.

## Practical bits

### 🚕 Taxis (rough, one-way)
- Mountcharles ↔ Donegal Town: ~€10-12
- Strandhill ↔ Sligo town: ~€15-20
- Teelin: walk to Rusty Mackerel (5 mins, no taxi needed)
- *Book return taxis in advance — rural Donegal/Sligo can be slow late at night*

### 📞 To book before we go
- **Slieve League shuttle** — book in advance for Wednesday, gets busy in July
- **Hargadon's table** for Friday dinner — packed for the trad night
- **Save campsite phone numbers** in case of late arrival:
  - Sliabh Liag Camping
  - Strandhill Caravan Park: +353 71 916 8111
  - Thelmount Camping (Mountcharles)

### 💶 Cash & ATMs
- Bank of Ireland in Donegal Town for cash before heading west
- ATMs get sparse west of Killybegs
- Maghera Caves parking is **cash only** (€3-4)

### ⛽ Fuel
- Top up in **Killybegs or Carrick** before heading to Teelin/Slieve League — last petrol stations
- Fuel up again in Donegal Town before the Sligo drive on Thursday

### 🅿️ Parking heads-ups
- **Maghera Beach** car park is small — arrive before midday or it fills up
- **Slieve League** — pay for the shuttle if it's busy, don't try to drive the narrow road up

### 🌊 Timing-sensitive
- **Tides for Wednesday:** check Tides Near Me app — Maghera Caves only work at low tide
- **Slieve League sunrise** ~5am Thursday — set an alarm, it's worth it
- **McLynn's** closed Mon/Tue — Thu/Fri perfect

### 🌧️ Weather
- Donegal in July: average ~16°C, **pack for 4 seasons in a day**
- Waterproofs, fleece, swim gear all needed
- Wet-weather backup: Donegal Town wandering, [Marble Arch Caves](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Marble+Arch+Caves+Fermanagh) in Fermanagh, or pubs (always)

### ⛺ Pack list essentials
- Midge spray (Donegal in July = midges, especially evenings near water)
- Waterproof jacket + trousers
- **Breathable eco groundsheet** if using an awning at Strandhill (mandatory — standard ones not allowed)
- Tide times app, headtorch, power bank
- Cash, hiking shoes, swim gear + towel
- Reusable water bottle (campsite taps are drinkable)

### 🎵 Music heads-up
South Sligo Summer School (massive trad festival) starts ~12 July, so Sligo will be buzzing on Friday — pubs will be busy, get there early.

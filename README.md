# 🏕️ Donegal + Sligo Camping Trip

**Mon 6 July → Sat 11 July 2026** · 5 nights, 2 of us, 1 car

---

## The plan in a sentence

Newbridge → Donegal Town (2 nights) → Slieve League cliffs (1 night) → Sligo (2 nights) → home. Days outdoors hiking, swimming and beach-hopping. Evenings in lively pub towns for trad music and pints.

## Day by day

### Mon 6 July — Newbridge → Donegal Town
- Drive up (~3.5 hrs)
- Lunch stop: **Tullan Strand & Fairy Bridges**, Bundoran — surf beach with sea arches, cliff walk
- Check in at **Thelmount Camping**, Mountcharles
- Evening: drive into Donegal Town for pints and trad at **The Reel Inn**

### Tue 7 July — Donegal Town day
- Wander Donegal Town: harbour, castle, Magee's tweed shop
- Afternoon walk: **Ardnamona Woods, Lough Eske** (2km woodland loop, lakeside)
- Dinner in town: **The Olde Castle Bar** (seafood) or back to The Reel Inn
- Sleep at Thelmount

### Wed 8 July — Donegal → Slieve League
- Pack up, drive west via Killybegs (fishing port, smoked salmon shops)
- **Maghera Beach & Caves** *(check tide times — low tide essential)*
- Check in at **Sliabh Liag Camping**, Teelin — at the foot of the cliffs
- Late afternoon: hike up to **Slieve League viewpoint** (highest sea cliffs in Europe, empty when day-trippers leave)
- Dinner: walk 5 mins to **Rusty Mackerel pub** — seafood, dog-friendly, locals' favourite

### Thu 9 July — The big day: Slieve League → Sligo
- ⏰ **Slieve League at sunrise** (~5am) — empty cliffs, the moment of the trip
- Breakfast, then drive to **Silver Strand at Malin Beg** — 169 steps to a hidden horseshoe beach
- Lunch at **Nancy's of Ardara** — 200-year-old pub, legendary seafood chowder
- Coast road south with quick stops:
  - **Gleniff Horseshoe** — drive-through valley ringed by cliffs
  - **Benbulben Forest Walk** — easy 5km loop *(skip if running late)*
  - **Glencar Waterfall** — Yeats's "Stolen Child" spot
- Check in at **Strandhill Caravan Park** (in the dunes by the surf beach)
- Evening: drive into Sligo town for trad at **McLynn's Bar**

### Fri 10 July — Knocknarea + Mullaghmore
- Morning hike up **Knocknarea** to Queen Maeve's cairn (~1 hr up, panoramic views)
- Afternoon: **Mullaghmore Head** loop drive, swim at the village beach
- Big night out in Sligo town:
  - Dinner at **Hargadon's** (book a table)
  - Trad sessions at **Thomas Connolly's** (the Friday seisiún is famous)
  - Then wander Castle/Bridge/O'Connell Streets — let the music pull you in
- Sleep at Strandhill

### Sat 11 July — Sligo → Newbridge
- ~3 hr drive home
- Optional Carrick-on-Shannon lunch stop
